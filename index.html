Our Story

Three Decades of
Smoke & Spice

Tucked away on School Lane in Nairobi, Nargis has been the city's best-kept secret for authentic Indian barbecue since the mid-nineties. What started as a humble grill shack has grown into a beloved institution — a place where locals and travellers from across the globe come for one thing: the unmistakable taste of charcoal-kissed tikka.

Our spice blends are crafted in-house. Our meat is marinated overnight. Our coals are lit the old way. No shortcuts, no compromises — just the kind of BBQ that makes you close your eyes on the first bite.
